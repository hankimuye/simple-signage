This plugin turns your wordpress installation into a digital signage solution.

== Description ==
With this plugin you can use build your own digitial signage solution.

It creates a new post-type signage and allow you to build your own slides in either portrait or landscape. 

Pick the duration of a slide and which transition should be used to transfer to the next slide. 

Open up your browser in kiosk mode and point it to domain.text/signage/ and your are ready.

== Installation ==

1. Go to the Add New plugins screen in your WordPress Dashboard
1. Click the upload tab
1. Browse for the plugin file (simple-signag.zip) on your computer
1. Click "Install Now" and then hit the activate button
